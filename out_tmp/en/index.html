About

Ph.D. in Engineering. Lecturer (2019–2024), Associate Professor (2024–). Funded by CSC for doctoral study, earned M.S. and Ph.D. from Waseda University in 2014 and 2019. Worked at MSRA, Toshiba Global R&D, and NTT Communication Science Labs. Over 30 papers in EMNLP, COLING, CogSci, etc., including 25+ in SCI/SSCI/A&HCI indexed journals or CCF venues. PI of Shanghai Sci&Tech Commission project (completed) and NSFC Youth program (AI-X), with 5 industry collaborations. Served as PC/Reviewer for ICLR, ICML, NeurIPS, ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, and journals such as TKDD, TNNLS, NLE. Research topics: AI, human-AI collaborative cognition, and NLP (incl. medical documents).

We welcome students with NLP backgrounds as well as those from linguistics, cognitive science, and psychology. International students are also welcome.
