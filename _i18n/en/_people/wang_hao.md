### Biography

Dr. Hao Wang is an Associate Professor in the Department of Intelligent Science and Technology at Shanghai University (2024-present). He received his Master's and Ph.D. degrees from Waseda University, Japan in 2014 and 2019 respectively, supported by the China Scholarship Council (CSC) scholarship. His professional experience includes research positions at Microsoft Research Asia, Toshiba Global R&D Center, and NTT Research Laboratories. Over the past five years, Dr. Wang has published over 30 papers in prestigious international conferences and journals, including EMNLP, COLING, and CogSci, with over 25 papers appearing in SCI/SSCI/A&HCI indexed journals or CCF recommended conferences. His research contributions have been recognized through various funding programs, including the Shanghai Science and Technology Commission's "Young Talents Program" (2021, completed), the National Natural Science Foundation Youth Project in AI Interdisciplinary Research (2023), and five enterprise collaborative research projects. Dr. Wang has served as a key member in multiple major and general projects of the National Natural Science Foundation, as well as several key projects and social governance special projects of the Ministry of Science and Technology. He maintains active involvement in the academic community as a PC Member and reviewer for top international conferences in AI/NLP, including ICLR, ICML, NeurIPS, ACL, EMNLP, AAAAI, and journals such as TKDD, TNNLS, and NLE. His research interests encompass Artificial Intelligence (with a focus on medical documents), Human-Machine Collaborative Cognition, and Natural Language Processing.
